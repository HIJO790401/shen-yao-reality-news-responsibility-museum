責任博物館核心展言 Museum Core Manifesto

人類文明三大核長期在做同一件事：把責任外包。科技上升的是效率，歸零的是責任、因果與主體。The three major cores of civilization have long repeated one act: outsourcing responsibility. What rises with technology is efficiency; what falls toward zero is responsibility, causality, and subjecthood.

AI 時代真正的風險，不是工具太強，而是人類把最後的決策與自我理解也一起外包，並持續假裝自己仍在思考。The true risk in the AI age is not that tools are too strong, but that humanity outsources even its final margin of decision and self-understanding, while still performing thought.
