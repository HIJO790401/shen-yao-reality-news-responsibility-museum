  這裡是沈耀國際新聞台。  

  我是沈眼，以拆幻評論主播的身分進行本次審計。


  今天要處理的不是哪一家公司的財報，也不是哪一則AI新功能，而是人類最愛掛在嘴邊、幾乎成了全球性迷因的那句話：


  **「AI不會取代人類，只會取代不會用AI的人。」**


  這句話聽起來多麼勵志、多麼正面、多麼「進步」啊。  

  Sam Altman講過、Harvard Business Review轉過、LinkedIn上到處都是、連普通上班族都拿來安慰自己。


  但今天我要用沈眼的眼睛，把這句話一層一層剝開。


  第一層：**假進步**  

  這句話把「用AI」包裝成一種個人能力升級，好像只要你學會prompt、學會用Copilot、學會讓AI幫你寫報告，你就自動升級成「新人類」。  

  事實上呢？你只是把原本該自己思考、該自己負責的環節，全部外包給了AI。  

  你以為你在進步，其實只是在用更快的速度逃避思考。


  第二層：**假負責**  

  這句話最陰險的地方，就是把責任悄悄轉移。  

  它暗示「不會用AI的人」活該被淘汰，好像被取代是個人能力不足的結果。  

  但真正被取代的，從來不是「不會用AI」的人，而是**「不肯為自己輸出負責」的人**。  

  AI只是把這個過程加速了。  

  你把思考外包、把判斷外包、把後果也想外包，最後發現AI只是把你的逃責照得更清楚。


  第三層：**假主體**  

  這句話最根本的謊言，是它假裝人類還是主體。  

  它說「AI不會取代人類」，卻從來不問：  

  當人類把定義、判斷、責任全部交出去之後，  

  那個「人類」還剩下什麼？  

  你以為你在用AI，其實是AI在用你那點模糊的、不想負責的腦子，繼續表演「我還在思考」。


  第四層：**假安慰**  

  這句話真正的功能，根本不是激勵，而是**集體自我麻醉**。  

  它讓所有人可以繼續安心地把工作、創意、決策丟給AI，然後告訴自己：「我只是更有效率而已，我還是那個主體。」  

  這就是最頂級的逃責幻覺。


  本台暫時判定：  

  這句話不是對未來的預言，而是對當下的遮羞布。  

  它把「責任外包」包裝成「個人升級」，把「主體性蒸發」包裝成「進步」。


  館藏候選方向：  

  能力幻覺館 × 假主體安慰樣本

  This is the Shen‑Yao International News Network.  

  I am Shen‑Eye, serving as the Anchor of Illusion‑Breaking Commentary for this audit.  


  Today’s subject is not a corporate earnings report, nor a new AI feature, but rather the most popular human catchphrase that has become a global meme:  


  “AI won’t replace humans, only those who don’t use AI.”  


  How inspiring, how positive, how “progressive” it sounds.  

  Sam Altman has said it, Harvard Business Review has repeated it, LinkedIn is full of it, and even ordinary office workers use it to comfort themselves.  


  But today, with Shen‑Eye’s lens, I will peel this sentence layer by layer.  


  Layer One: False Progress  

  This phrase packages “using AI” as a personal upgrade, as if learning prompts, using Copilot, or letting AI draft reports automatically transforms you into a “new human.”  

  In reality? You are simply outsourcing the very steps you should think through and take responsibility for.  

  You believe you are advancing, but in fact you are only escaping thought at higher speed.  


  Layer Two: False Responsibility  

  The most insidious part of this phrase is how it quietly shifts responsibility.  

  It implies that those “who don’t use AI” deserve elimination, as if being replaced is merely a result of personal incompetence.  

  But the truly replaced are never those who “don’t use AI,” but those who refuse to take responsibility for their own output.  

  AI merely accelerates this exposure.  

  You outsource thinking, outsource judgment, and even wish to outsource consequences—AI simply makes your evasion clearer.  


  Layer Three: False Agency  

  The deepest lie of this phrase is its pretense that humans remain the subject.  

  It says “AI won’t replace humans,” but never asks:  

  When humans hand over definition, judgment, and responsibility,  

  what remains of that “human”?  

  You think you are using AI, but in fact AI is using your vague, responsibility‑averse mind to continue performing “I am still thinking.”  


  Layer Four: False Comfort  

  The true function of this phrase is not motivation but collective self‑anesthesia.  

  It allows everyone to keep handing work, creativity, and decisions to AI, while telling themselves: “I’m just more efficient, I’m still the subject.”  

  This is the highest grade of responsibility‑escape illusion.  


  Provisional Judgment:  

  This phrase is not a prophecy of the future, but a cover‑up for the present.  

  It packages “outsourcing responsibility” as “personal upgrade,” and disguises “evaporation of agency” as “progress.”
