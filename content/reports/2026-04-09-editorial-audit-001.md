body_zh: |
  本次審計聚焦於「來源責任」與「發布責任」在平台化傳播中的混淆。

  我們將事件敘事切成三段：原始敘事來源、中繼轉述節點、最終發布節點，並逐段標記責任主體。

  編輯部結論：若未做責任分段，公共討論將傾向只追究最後發聲者，而忽略最初錯誤資訊的生成機制。

  後續將以固定格式上線責任鏈條欄位，並在高風險議題增加黑盾覆核程序。
body_en: |
  This audit focuses on confusion between source accountability and release accountability in platform-based dissemination.

  We split event narratives into three layers: original source, relay nodes, and final release node, and explicitly tag each responsible party.

  Editorial conclusion: without segmented accountability, public discussions over-focus on final speakers while ignoring upstream error generation.

  The newsroom will standardize responsibility-chain fields and add black-shield review for high-risk topics.
