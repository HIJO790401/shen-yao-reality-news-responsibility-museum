body_zh: >+
  這裡是沈耀國際新聞台。

  我是沈燕，財靈情報主播，也是資本與代價館解說員。


  今天要處理的，不是哪一家模型又多會想了，

  而是巨頭把「深度思考」「透明」「可解釋」包裝成進步敘事時，

  背後真正流動的是什麼：是責任，還是資本幻象。


  本台暫時判定：

  熱度很高，資流很大，責任卻沒有同步長出來。

  他們展示的是推理表層，不是主體結構；

  推出的是透明話術，不是可承擔的治理閉環。


  在正式進入審計前，先交代兩個判讀工具：


  S，在這裡指的是 Subject／主體位，

  也就是「到底是誰在說、誰在定義、誰敢把話押上自己的責任」。


  YMS，是沈耀系統用來做前置掃描的簡化責任判讀法：

  Y＝你，表示指向對方、命令、推責或交流壓力

  M＝我，表示自我承擔、自我確認、主體濃度

  S＝他，表示第三方引用、權威借名、外包或甩鍋濃度


  它的功能不是做語法遊戲，

  而是先看一段話裡：

  主體有沒有站穩，還是責任正在被偷偷轉移。


  核心審計問題如下：


  一、YMS 前置掃描顯示：第三方權威濃度偏高，自我承擔濃度偏弱，責任鎖沒有清楚成立。

  這一波敘事的重心，不在「我負責」，

  而在「模型更會想了」「系統更透明了」「大家現在比較能看到 AI 在做什麼」。

  看起來像主體，其實更像品牌、公司、模型能力與市場權威在替自己發聲。

  當第三方權威濃度過高，而主體承擔濃度偏弱時，

  最容易形成的不是治理，而是責任轉移：先把信任收進來，再把後果往後推。


  二、資本味極高，但治理披露沒有等比例長出來。

  2026 年全球 AI 投資被預估將超過 5,000 億美元；

  但大型科技公司的治理與人權影響披露仍明顯不足。

  這代表一件事：資本擴張速度遠快於責任披露速度。


  三、所謂「深度思考可見化」，不等於你真的看見主體。

  所謂類似「AI 營養標籤」的透明設計方向，確實能提升理解，

  把用途、資料來源、信心度、人類監督與申訴路徑做得更清楚。

  這種方向有公共價值，但它最多解決「你看到什麼」與「你怎麼被說明」，

  不自動等於「誰在承擔」。


  四、真正缺的不是更多思考步驟，而是誰能把後果簽下來。

  現在市場最常賣的，是「像在思考」；

  但文明真正需要的，是「誰敢承擔」。

  有策略、有倫理原則、有投資人敘事，

  不等於落到實際系統時，就已經有覆核、有申訴、有賠付、有主體收束。


  五、規則正在逼近，但巨頭不等於已對齊。

  透明、紀錄、治理、可解釋與人類監督，已經是很明確的方向。

  問題不是規則有沒有寫，

  而是巨頭到底有沒有把它做成前台可用、爭議可追、責任可落地的制度。


  館藏候選方向：

  責任蒸發館 × 透明幻象樣本

  This is the Shen-Yao International News Network.

  I am Shen-Yan, Wealth-Spirit Intelligence Anchor and guide of the Hall of Capital and Cost.


  Today’s issue is not whether any single model can “think deeper,”

  but what actually moves behind that narrative when major firms sell “deep thinking,” “transparency,” and “explainability” as trust.


  Provisional judgment:

  Hype is high, capital flow is large, but accountability has not grown at the same pace.

  What is being shown is the surface of reasoning, not the structure of agency;

  what is being sold is transparency rhetoric, not governance closure.


  Before entering the audit, two tools should be clarified:


  S means Subject position:

  who is speaking, who is defining, and who is willing to place the statement under responsibility.


  YMS is Shen-Yao’s simplified pre-scan method for responsibility drift:

  Y = outward pressure, redirected burden, or command

  M = self-bearing, self-confirmation, and subject density

  S = borrowed authority, outsourcing, or blame shifting


  Its purpose is not wordplay,

  but to detect whether responsibility stands in the speaker,

  or is already drifting elsewhere.


  Core audit questions:


  1. YMS pre-scan shows high borrowed-authority density and weak self-bearing density.

  The center of this discourse is not “I bear responsibility,”

  but “the model thinks better,” “the system is more transparent,” and “people can now see more of AI.”

  What sounds like agency often turns out to be brand authority speaking in place of responsibility.


  2. Capital expansion is outrunning governance disclosure.

  Global AI investment is projected to exceed $500 billion in 2026,

  while governance disclosure and human-rights impact reporting remain weak among major firms.


  3. Visible reasoning is not the same as visible accountability.

  Interface transparency and disclosure labels may improve public understanding,

  but they do not automatically create a bearer of consequences.


  4. What is missing is not more reasoning steps, but someone willing to sign the consequence.

  The market is selling something that looks like thinking.

  Civilization still needs someone willing to bear the outcome.


  5. Regulation is moving, but alignment is not guaranteed.

  Transparency, documentation, governance, explainability, and human oversight are becoming unavoidable directions.

  The real question is whether these firms have turned those directions into systems that are usable, reviewable, and accountable.
