  跨語境報導不只是語言轉換，更是責任結構轉換。

  本篇提出三項準則：

  二、評論與事實必須分欄；

body_en: |
  Cross-context reporting is not only language conversion but accountability-structure conversion.

  This report introduces three standards:
  1) do not separate actors from actions,
  2) separate commentary from factual statements,
  3) attach original narrative sources to translated copies.

  Future reports will include bilingual key-term mapping for traceability and public readability.
